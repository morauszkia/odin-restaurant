Trattoria Eroilor

Experience the flavors of the Italian countryside in the heart of Budapest

Situated near the iconic Heroes' Square, our restaurant offers an authentic Italian dining experience. Our chefs meticulously craft each dish using the finest, high-quality ingredients imported directly from Italy. From freshly made pasta to rich, flavorful sauces, every bite is a celebration of traditional Italian cuisine. Join us for a culinary journey that captures the essence of Italy, right here in Budapest.
